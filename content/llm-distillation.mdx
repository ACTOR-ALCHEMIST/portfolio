The useful part of a distillation project is not only the final metric. It is the discipline of defining examples, failure modes, evaluation prompts, and demo flows that make model behavior visible.

For mathematical reasoning tasks, I usually separate three layers: data preparation, teacher response design, and student model evaluation. Keeping those layers separate makes later debugging much easier.
